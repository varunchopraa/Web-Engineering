UEFA Champions League

The UEFA Champions League is an annual club football competition organised by the Union of European Football Associations and contested by top-division European clubs, deciding the best team in Europe.

  • Number of teams: 32 (group stage); 79 (total)
  • Most successful club(s): Real Madrid (13 titles)
  • Qualifier for: UEFA Super Cup; FIFA Club World Cup
  • Current champion: Liverpool F.C. (6th title)
  • Venues: Wanda Metropolitano Stadium, Milan
